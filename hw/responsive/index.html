KNK (크나큰) is a Korean band that currently consists of 5 members: Jihun, Seoham, Dongwon, Inseong and Heejun. The group debuted on February 29, 2016 under YNB Entertainment.

KNK, also known as Keunakeun (크나큰) meaning “great” or “to be great”, is a five member boy group from YNB entertainment, home of groups such as Bestie and Noel. Each member trained with a different company before joining YNB entertainment. They have made appearances in music videos for with their lablemate Bestie and have performed as their backup dancers during promotions. With an average height of 184.8cm (6'1"), they are currently one of the tallest active boy groups in Korea.
